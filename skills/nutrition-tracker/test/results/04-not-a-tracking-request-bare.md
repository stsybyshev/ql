# Simple Aglio e Olio (Garlic and Oil Pasta)

This is quick, delicious, and needs just a few pantry staples:

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
- 6-8 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- ½ cup good olive oil
- ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley (optional)
- Parmesan cheese

**Instructions:**
1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente
2. While pasta cooks, gently heat olive oil with garlic and red pepper flakes over medium heat until garlic is golden (don't burn it)
3. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain pasta
4. Toss pasta with the garlic oil, adding pasta water a bit at a time to create a light sauce
5. Season with salt, pepper, and top with parmesan and parsley

**Total time:** ~20 minutes
